Overview
Ganyu (Chinese: 甘雨 Gānyǔ) is a playable Cryo character in Genshin Impact.
She is a half-qilin adeptus under contract with the Geo Archon Morax and serves as the general secretary of the Liyue Qixing. Due to her adeptal background, she finds herself feeling separated from the human world.
